Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Thank you for choosing to be part of our community by downloading Kompari ("App"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy.

This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and disclosed when you use the Kompari mobile application.

1. Information Collection and Use

Kompari does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information. You can use the core features of the App completely anonymously. We do not require you to create an account, nor do we ask for your name, email address, or phone number.

All pricing and product comparison data you enter into the App is processed locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

2. Third-Party Services (AdMob)

While the App itself does not collect personal data, we use Google AdMob to display advertisements that keep the App free to use.

Google AdMob is a third-party service that may collect and use certain data automatically to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. The information collected by Google AdMob may include:

  • Device information (such as device model, operating system, and hardware identifiers)
  • IP addresses
  • Mobile advertising identifiers (such as the Android Advertising ID)
  • App usage and interaction data related to ad performance

To learn more about how Google collects and uses your data, please review the Google Privacy & Terms.

3. Permissions

Kompari requires minimal device permissions to function. We do not request access to your camera, contacts, location, microphone, or storage.

4. Children’s Privacy

Our App does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. Because we do not collect personal data directly, no such data is stored by us. However, our third-party advertising partner (Google AdMob) may collect data as outlined in their own privacy policies.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
